About the Neuromorphic Research Center

The Neuromorphic Research Center at the University of Peradeniya is dedicated to advancing the science and engineering of brain-inspired computing. Our team of researchers, engineers, and students explores the intersection of neuroscience, artificial intelligence, and hardware design to create systems that emulate the efficiency and adaptability of the human brain.
